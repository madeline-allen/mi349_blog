About the author

Madeline Allen is a Lebanese-American undergraduate student studying Media and Information at Michigan State University. Madeline aims to lead a career in User Experience Research and Design. Madeline is an Events Director and Staff Writer for HER Campus.

In her free time, Madeline enjoys various forms of artistic expression. She regularly practices photography, writing, and drawing. She will often write poetry or fictional pieces and gift them to people that have inspired her writing. Madeline feels that she is most creative late at night.
